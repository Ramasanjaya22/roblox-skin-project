## Pricing Strategy

### Recommended Price Points
| Item Type | Robux Price | Rationale |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| Basic accessory | 25-50 | High volume |
| Mid-tier item | 100-250 | Good margin |
| Premium/Limited | 500-2000 | Collector market |

### Revenue Calculation
```
Your Share = Price × 30%

Example:
- Price: 100 Robux
- Sales: 200 units/month
- Revenue: 100 × 200 × 30% = 6,000 Robux
- Approx: Rp 200.000-300.000/month
```
