# Tools & Resources

## Essential Tools

### 3D Modeling
| Tool | Cost | Use Case |
|------|------|-----------|
| Blender | Free | 3D modeling, rigging, animation |
| Maya | Paid ($25/mo) | Professional workflows |
| ZBrush | Paid ($30/mo) | Sculpting |

### 2D/Texturing
| Tool | Cost | Use Case |
|------|------|-----------|
| Photoshop | Paid | Texture creation |
| Procreate | $12.99 (iPad) | Concept art |
| GIMP | Free | Free Photoshop alternative |
| Krita | Free | Digital painting |

### Design/UI
| Tool | Cost | Use Case |
|------|------|-----------|
| Figma | Free tier | UI mockups |
| Canva | Free tier | Quick designs |
